The best movies according to Paul

My 3 favourite movies of all time...

Gladiator

I love this movie because it reminds me of my childhood. A great childhood...

Interstellar

I love this movie because it portrays some objective and cold facts about the physical nature of reality, but at the same time conveying an immense level of emotional depth...

Avatar

Also a great movie from my middle childhood. It has great visuals, a great story and message, a very evocative soundtrack. I love it. I've watched it like 10 times already since it went out in 2009...
